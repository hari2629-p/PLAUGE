Title: Computer Networks

Source: crossref
Authors: Mrs. D. S. Jayakumari
Year: 2024
URL: https://doi.org/10.59646/cn/283

Abstract:
This comprehensive textbook on Computer Networks provides an in-depth exploration of the fundamental concepts, architectures, and technologies that underpin modern networking. With a focus on both theoretical foundations and practical applications, this book covers the entire spectrum of computer networking, from local area networks to wide area networks, wireless networks, and the Internet.
